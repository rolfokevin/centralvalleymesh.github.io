About this site

This site is community-maintained and open source. If something is wrong, outdated, or missing — you can fix it. Check the Contribute Guide to get started, or flag it in #website on Discord.

Privacy & Safety

Central Valley Mesh does not collect personal data. Nodes on the network transmit radio packets only — no cameras, no microphones, no location tracking of visitors. See the Found a Node page for more.
