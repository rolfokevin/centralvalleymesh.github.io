// How to Contribute

Every node helps.

01

Deploy a Node

The biggest single contribution. A rooftop node in router mode relays messages for your whole area and extends coverage for everyone on the network.

02

Share Your Build

Document what you built — hardware list, photos, lessons learned. It helps the next person trying something similar.

03

Help in Discord

Answer questions, share signal reports, troubleshoot with others. Active members make this a place worth joining for newcomers.

04

Improve the Docs

See something outdated? Open a GitHub PR or flag it in #website on Discord. Fresh eyes always catch things.

// Community Guidelines

Keep it friendly. Keep it clean.

These apply in Discord, on the mesh network, and all community spaces.

Not Allowed

No Politics or Religion

This network is for everyone — leave divisive topics out of community channels.

Not Allowed

No Foul Language

Use respectful language at all times. Offensive language is not tolerated anywhere.

Not Allowed

No SHAT Content

Sex, Hate, Alcohol, Tobacco/Drugs. Family-friendly for all ages at all times.

Not Allowed

No Commercial Spam

No ads or unsolicited promotions. Community spaces are not a marketplace.

Required

Legal Node Placement

Only place nodes where you have permission. No rogue installations — ever.

Required

All-Age Appropriate

If you wouldn't say it with a kid in the room, don't say it on the mesh or in Discord.

// California Meshtastic Communities

We're part of something bigger.

Active mesh communities running across California — from the Bay Area to San Diego. We share knowledge, techniques, and a mutual interest in keeping the network growing. Listed north to south.

All Global Groups
Northern California
BAY

Bay Mesh — SF Bay Area

"The Best Mesh in the West." One of the largest CA groups, covering the Bay Area with strong infrastructure, solid docs, and an active community.

bayme.sh Map Discord
SAC

Sac Valley Mesh — Sacramento

Serving Sacramento, Yolo, and surrounding counties. Closely adjacent to our network — nodes from both groups can overlap in coverage.

sacvalleymesh.com Map Discord
Central Valley
CVM

Central Valley Mesh — You Are Here

Stockton, Manteca, Modesto, and Tracy as our primary coverage area. Members from Turlock, Merced, Fresno, and beyond are active in our Discord.

centralvalleymesh.net Map Discord
VM

Visalia Mesh — Visalia Area

Sister community to the south — Visalia, Tulare, and surrounding areas. Active Discord, node map, and growing infrastructure in the southern valley.

visaliamesh.com
Southern California
SCAL

SoCal Mesh — LA, OC & San Diego

Hundreds of nodes across Los Angeles, Orange County, Inland Empire, and San Diego. One of the most active Meshtastic communities in the entire state.

socalmesh.org
MV

Mission Viejo Mesh — OC / Laguna

Community building mesh coverage in Mission Viejo and the Laguna area of Orange County, connecting into the broader SoCal network.

missionviejomesh.org

Running a mesh community in California?

If your group isn't listed here, submit a pull request to get added. It takes about 5 minutes — just copy an existing community card, fill in your details, and open a PR on GitHub.
